# PRICING PHILOSOPHY

When pricing this type of project, consider:
1. **Value delivered** - What would they pay for equivalent commercial solutions?
2. **Your expertise** - Custom development, AI integration, DevOps skills
3. **Ongoing responsibility** - Hosting, maintenance, support
4. **Market rates** - What agencies charge for similar work
5. **Client budget** - Real estate typically has good margins

# CONTRACT CONSIDERATIONS

## Include in Agreement:

1. **Scope Definition**
   - Exactly what's included
   - What constitutes "changes" vs "new development"
   - Number of revision rounds

2. **Payment Terms**
   - 50% upfront, 50% on completion
   - Monthly hosting billed in advance
   - Late payment terms

3. **Ownership**
   - Client owns their data
   - Client owns custom content
   - You retain rights to underlying code/frameworks

4. **Support Boundaries**
   - Response times
   - Hours of availability
   - What's included vs. billable

5. **Termination**
   - 30-day notice for monthly
   - Data export provided
   - Transition assistance (billable)
